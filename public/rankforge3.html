Common NAP Inconsistency Issues & How to Fix Them
❌ Phone Variations
Same number formatted differently: (512) 555-0100 vs 512-555-0100 vs 5125550100. Google treats these as different data points.
Fix: Standardise to (XXX) XXX-XXXX across all directories.
⚠ Address Variants
Street vs St vs St. — Suite 100 vs Ste 100 vs #100. These all signal different locations to Google.
Fix: Use USPS format — match exactly what appears on your Google Business Profile.
ℹ Name Variations
Acme Plumbing vs Acme Plumbing Co vs Acme Plumbing Company. Inconsistent names dilute citation authority.
Fix: Use the exact GBP name — no trademarked symbols unless on GBP itself.
